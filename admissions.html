Admissions 2026-27

Begin your academic journey at Chaudhary Devi Lal University. Applications are open for UG, PG, diploma, and PhD programmes.

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for admission to CDLU.

1

Online Registration

Visit the university portal and create your account. Provide basic personal details and create login credentials.

2

Fill Application Form

Complete the online application form with your academic qualifications, programme preferences, and personal information.

3

Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of required documents including photographs, signatures, mark sheets, and certificates.

4

Pay Application Fee

Pay the application fee online through net banking, debit card, credit card, or UPI as per programme requirements.

5

Merit List & Counselling

Merit lists will be published based on qualifying examination marks. Selected candidates proceed to counselling.

6

Enrolment & Fee Payment

Confirm your seat by paying the admission fee and completing the enrolment formalities online.

Eligibility Requirements

Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria before applying.

Undergraduate Eligibility

  • 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board with minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories)
  • Subject-specific requirements as per programme (e.g., Science with Mathematics for B.Tech, Arts for BA)
  • Valid score in any national/state-level entrance test where applicable

Postgraduate Eligibility

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant discipline from a recognized university with minimum 50% marks
  • Valid score in any national/state-level entrance test (if applicable)
  • Work experience may be considered for professional programmes like MBA

PhD & M.Phil Eligibility

  • Master's degree in relevant discipline with minimum 55% marks (50% for reserved categories)
  • UGC NET/CSIR NET/JRF qualified or university entrance exam
  • Interview and presentation of research proposal
