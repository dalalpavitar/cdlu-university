Welcome to CDLU

Chaudhary Devi Lal University is a premier state university in Sirsa, Haryana, committed to academic excellence and holistic development.

🎓

Quality Education

Offering 37+ academic programmes across UG, PG, diploma, and doctoral levels through 24 specialized teaching departments.

📚

Diverse Programs

Programmes in Science, Arts, Commerce, Law, Engineering, Management, Education, Journalism, and more under one roof.

🌎

Sprawling Campus

A 213-acre campus with modern infrastructure including 4 teaching blocks, Vivekananda Library, hostels, and sports facilities.

🔬

Research & Innovation

Connected to National Knowledge Network via 1 GBPS optical fiber. Active research across sciences, social sciences, and humanities.

🏫

Campus Facilities

Wi-Fi campus, well-equipped labs, health centre, bank with ATM, shopping complex, and community radio station.

🏆

UGC Recognized

Recognized by UGC under Section 12(B) and accredited for central assistance. Affiliated with 62+ colleges across the region.
